4. What an "API" or "Endpoint" means (Your Web Doorbell)
At the end of the project, you will build a Flask API.
When people hear "API", they think it's complicated. It's not.

Your Python script is your house.

Your trained model is the person sitting inside the house.

The API is just the doorbell.
Someone on the internet rings the doorbell (sends a request) and says: "House has 6 rooms, built in 1990, income 50k."
Your Flask app (the doorman) takes that message, gives it to the model inside, gets the price prediction, and hands it back to the person at the door.
