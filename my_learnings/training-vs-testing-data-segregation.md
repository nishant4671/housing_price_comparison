. "Training" vs. "Testing" (The School Exam Analogy)
The AI is going to split your data into two groups: Training Set and Test Set.

Training Set (80%): This is your model's "textbook." You show it the house details AND the correct prices so it can learn the patterns.

Test Set (20%): This is the "final exam." You hide the prices. You ask the model to predict the prices for these houses, and then you check its answers against the real, hidden prices.
If it does well on the exam, you know it actually learned the pattern (instead of just memorizing the textbook).
