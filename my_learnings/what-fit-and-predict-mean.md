3. What "Fit" and "Predict" mean (Your two magic words)
When you write your Python code, you will literally type these two words:

model.fit(X_train, y_train) -> "Hey model, study this textbook (X_train) and these correct answers (y_train). Learn the pattern."

model.predict(X_test) -> "Hey model, you studied. Now take this new test data (X_test) and tell me what you think the prices are."

You type those two lines, and Python does billions of calculations in half a second to give you an answer.
